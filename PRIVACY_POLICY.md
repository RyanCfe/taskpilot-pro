# Privacy Policy for TaskPilot Pro

TaskPilot Pro is a Chrome extension that helps users find ranked AI tools, copy prompts, and launch workflows faster.

## Data Collection

TaskPilot Pro does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal user data.

## Local Storage

TaskPilot Pro stores the following information locally in the user's browser:

- saved favorite tasks/prompts
- launch checklist completion status
- temporary prompts created from right-click selected text

This data stays on the user's device through Chrome local storage.

## External Websites

TaskPilot Pro opens external websites when the user clicks a tool, such as ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Canva, TradingView, and others.

Those websites have their own privacy policies and terms.

## No Tracking

TaskPilot Pro does not use:

- analytics
- advertising trackers
- cookies
- backend servers
- user accounts
- browsing history collection
