# Privacy Policy for TaskPilot Pro

TaskPilot Pro does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal user data.

## Local Storage

TaskPilot Pro stores only:
- saved favorite tasks/prompts
- launch checklist completion status
- temporary prompts generated from right-click selected text

All of this remains locally in the user's browser through Chrome local storage.

## External Websites

When the user clicks a tool, TaskPilot Pro opens external websites such as ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Canva, TradingView, and others. Those websites have their own privacy policies.

## No Tracking

TaskPilot Pro has:
- no backend server
- no analytics
- no ad tracking
- no account system
